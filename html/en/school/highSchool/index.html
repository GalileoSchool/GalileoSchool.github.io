    Best in Europe

    One of our outstanding students has been named a Top in Region winner in the OxfordAQA Go Further Awards 2024. This recognition, awarded by OxfordAQA, celebrates academic excellence and exceptional achievement among international students. Being named Top in Region places our student among the highest performers across Europe, reflecting their dedication, talent, and hard work.

    The Go Further Awards are a prestigious benchmark of success, identifying students who have gone above and beyond in their academic journey. This accomplishment not only highlights the student's personal commitment but also showcases the high standards of teaching and support within our school community. We extend our heartfelt congratulations and look forward to celebrating more successes like this in the future.

    Third Grade Rambles Through Japan

    Fifteen students from the 3rd B class (GYM3B) of our high school embarked on an unforgettable trip to Japan from February 28 to March 9, 2025. During their six nights in Tokyo and three in Kyoto, the students had a unique opportunity to experience the fascinating blend of modern technology and the rich culture of this Asian country.

    In Tokyo, they visited significant places such as Kawasaki Robospace, the Museum of Emerging Science, and the Maglev Museum, where they explored the latest technological innovations. The cultural aspect of the trip was enriched by visits to landmarks like the Meiji Jingu Shrine, the Inari Shrine, and several Buddhist temples. This trip not only provided our students with valuable educational experiences but also gave them a deeper understanding of Japanese culture and traditions, leaving them with unforgettable memories and valuable insights.
